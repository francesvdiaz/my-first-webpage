"MISSING"

My hopes and dreams...

I was walking home at midnight and I heard your voice,

whispering...

calling...

Softened conditioned, falling apart

Loose threads, my memories in the blazing sun-

Slowly, coming closely,

Sweetly; sweet, sweet still and calming.
